Six Departments. One Roof.

Every specialty at Meridian operates to the same standard — unhurried appointments and continuity of care.

🩺

General & Internal Medicine

Our largest department handles everything from annual health checks to the ongoing management of chronic conditions. It's usually the first point of contact for new patients, and where most long-term care relationships begin.

Conditions Treated
  • Annual health assessments
  • Diabetes and thyroid management
  • Hypertension
  • Travel health and vaccinations
  • Specialist referrals
What to Expect
  • Minimum 30-minute consultation
  • Full history review at first visit
  • Digital record shared across departments
Initial: $120–180Follow-up: $80–120

Cardiology

Our cardiology team combines diagnostics and ongoing management under one roof, so a suspicious reading doesn't mean a referral chain — it means an answer, usually the same day.

Conditions Treated
  • Hypertension
  • Arrhythmia
  • Cardiac risk screening
  • Post-event monitoring
What to Expect
  • ECG and echocardiogram on-site
  • Holter monitoring available
  • Results reviewed same visit where possible
