Common Questions

Insurance FAQ

Do I need pre-authorisation from my insurer?

It depends on your policy and the treatment. We check this for you and handle the pre-authorisation request directly with your insurer where required.

What if my insurer isn't on your list?

We can often still bill directly. Contact us with your insurer's details before your appointment and we'll confirm.

Will I need to pay anything upfront?

Insured patients typically pay nothing upfront beyond any excess or copay specified in their policy.

Can I get an invoice for reimbursement?

Yes. Detailed invoices are issued the same day and are suitable for insurance reimbursement claims.

Is my data shared with my insurer without consent?

No. We only share the information required to process a claim, and always with your consent.
