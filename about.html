Our Story

Founded by Physicians, for Patients

Meridian Health Clinic was founded in 2012 by a team of physicians who wanted to practise medicine the way they had trained to — without the time pressure and administrative overhead of the public hospital system.

We are a private, independent clinic. We are not part of any hospital chain, and we answer to no shareholders. Every clinical decision here is made by clinicians, not administrators, which is a distinction that shapes everything from appointment length to how we bill.
